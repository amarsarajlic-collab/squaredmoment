When you access the site, the hosting provider automatically processes technical access data (e.g. IP address, date and time of access, browser type, requested file), as is standard for any web server. Fonts

If you contact me via the e-mail link, your message is sent directly through your own e-mail client. No data is stored or processed on this website itself — communication takes place exclusively between you and me by e-mail.
